# Tizi

**Senior UX Designer · Design Strategist · Product Thinker**  
Based in Stuttgart · Working across automotive, mobility, and digital product contexts

---

## What I do

I design the systems that sit between complex organizations and the people who use their products — interfaces where enterprise logic has to become human logic, fast.

My scope spans B2C, B2B, and fleet management surfaces, typically within large-scale automotive and mobility platforms where technical constraints are real, stakeholder environments are layered, and the cost of getting interaction patterns wrong is high.

Increasingly, my work sits at the edge of UX and product strategy: not just defining how things should look and behave, but why specific design decisions hold under pressure — and how to communicate that reasoning to the people who will challenge it.

---

## How I work

**I treat design rationale as a deliverable, not a footnote.**  
Every significant design decision has a traceable argument — grounded in user behavior, business constraints, and systemic coherence. This matters especially in environments where design is questioned by engineers, product owners, and stakeholders simultaneously.

**I design for information density without cognitive overload.**  
My component philosophy: Clarity through structure, not decoration, with a focus on consistent behavior across viewports. Outside of the constraints of following a corporate identity, 

**I work at the intersection of human-centered design and AI-augmented process.**  
Nowadays, I actively integrate AI into the design workflow — Not only as a productivity shortcut, but as a way to expose my mind to solutions that would have gone undiscovered otherwise.
